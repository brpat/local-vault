Vault Overview

Local Vault is a powerful and private password management solution built on the secure Django web framework. By hosting Local Vault yourself, you gain complete control over your password data. Effortlessly store, organize, and retrieve your logins, knowing your sensitive information is protected on your own server.

Key Features

  • Securely store your website credentials
  • Easy access to your usernames and passwords
  • Basic notes for each entry
  • Secure password generation
